Taxi2Gate – Paris CDG First Preview

BY Sebastien, Tuesday, 20 December 2016 @ 16:32

Taxi2Gate is used to leak the first shots of their projects sceneries only when they are close to release. And about Paris Charles De Gaulle (LFPG), the first preview has been posted in their forum, that needs a free registration.

Featuring 3 terminals, with wide maintenance and cargo areas, Air France and Aeroports de Paris headquarters, the TGV high speed train station, it is the kind of project that needs some time to get modelled and detailed. Taxi2Gate adds it could be “ready this year”.
